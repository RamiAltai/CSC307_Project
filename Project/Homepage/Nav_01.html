Our Vision

Create a network between elite professional alumni across a multitude of fields and a promising select of students, who share a great understanding of IoT(internet of things)/technology and general knowledge of fields that are not necessarily their own, to improve and inspire originality in conceptual design, skill development and ideas implementation.

Our Plan

A significant portion of the society funds will go into providing general tools, equipment, software license and other tools that may help members in academia or creative endeavors, while the majority will be used to handle event organization costs among other responsibilities. The society may allocate rewards for wining in certain events.
